Absa Online Figma Library

As part of a brand update at Absa, we were assigned the task of updating the Absa Online Figma library. This included defining component variant states that were previously incomplete. The new library ensures that colour contrast ratios comply with AA WCAG standards, and it introduces screen reader content along with accessibility guidance for each component.
